Myers Hall

Location: South Campus
Recommended Bus Stop: Soule Hall/ Myers Quad

Myers Hall is part of the Myers Community along with Mary Lyndon, Rutherford, and Soule Halls. Myers Hall contains the mailrooms for all of the Myers Community as well as the 24-Hour assistance desk. This hall has three wheelchair accessible entrances. The building also houses (but is not restricted to) students in the honors program.
Resources to be found in Myers Hall include package pickup and mailboxes for residents of Myers Community, as well as the Myers Community office and 24-hour desk.

Quick Information

  • Automatic Door Entrance:
    From bus stop- After getting off the bus, find the ramp by the left staircase. Continue on the sidewalk to either the right or left door. Both of these are wheelchair accessible.
    From S. Lumpkin St (heading south, back entrance)- Follow the sidewalk to find a ramp on your left that leads to the accessible door in the center.
  • Service Desk:
    From bus stop- Enter the building to the lobby. Locate the desk to the right for assistance.
    From S. Lumpkin St- Enter the building to the lobby. Locate the desk to the left for assistance.
  • Community Desk:
    From bus stop- Enter the building to the lobby. Past the assistance desk, follow the ramp up to the hallway on the right, then turn at the first door to the left.
    From S. Lumpkin St- Enter the building to the lobby. Follow the small ramp up to the hallway on the left, then turn at the first door to the left.
  • Elevators: To reach other floors, you must be able to access dorms by entering a keycode at the hallway door. The elevator will then be located to your right (North Wing) or left (South Wing).
  • Accessible Restrooms: (From bus stop entrance doors) Through the lobby, enter the left (South) hallway. Up the ramp, locate the restrooms to your left.
