Science Learning Center

Location: South Campus
Recommended Bus Stop: Science Learning Center (SLC) / Stegman Coliseum

The Science Learning Center is a large lecture hall. It can be easily spotted from Carlton Street, across from Stegman Coliseum. The building has three entrances, each of which has a ramp and accessible door. The entrance closest to the recommended bus stop will have elevators located down the left hall after entering hte building. The grand entrance and the right side entrance (closer to Pharmacy South building) will have elevators to the right. More details on this are listed below images.
Resources at the Science Learning Center include an Einstein's Bagels/Caribou Coffee, group/TA meeting rooms, open seating space for causal studying or breaks, chemistry labs, a ScaleUp Classroom, and large lecture halls.

Quick Information

  • Automatic Door Entrance: Button located to the right, from any entrance.
  • Service Desk: Past seating area, to the left (from main entrance).
  • Elevator:
    From bus stop entrance- After passing through the two sets of double doors, follow the first hallway on your left, pass the coffee stand, pass the staircase downward, the elevator will be on your right.
    From right entrance (closest to Pharmacy South)- After passing through two sets of double doors, continue down the walkway past the seating area. To the right of the staircase leading upward, there are two elevators.
    From main entrance- After passing through two sets of double doors, pass the seating areas and turn right to the first hallway. Down this hallway, pass the coffee shop and the stairs leading downward. The elevator is located to the right.
  • Accessible Restrooms: Main floor: (from main entrance doors) Make a right down the first hallway, located on the right past the coffee shop, stairs, and elevators. Women's restroom is on the right past vending machines as well.
