Snelling Dining Commons

Location: South Campus
Recommended Bus Stop: Snelling / Georgia Center

The Snelling Dining Commons is known for its 24/4 availability. Food options include pizza, pasta, grill food, classic hispanic foods, hot and cold sandwiches, soup, salad bar, special item of the day, and breakfast selections including cereal, waffles, eggs, bacon, breads, sausage, muffins, and more.
The basement of Snelling Dining Commons also features a study nook, several couches, and accessible restrooms, as well as a wheelchair accesible entrance to the food services office.

Dining Hours

  • Monday-Thursday: 24 Hours
  • Friday: 12:00AM-2:30PM
  • Saturday-Sunday: Closed

    • Quick Information

    • Automatic Door Entrance: Up the ramp located several feet to the right of the stairs, from outside entrance.
    • Dining entrance: Enter through turnstyles to the right upon entering the lobby.
    • Elevator: To reach other floors, (from accessibility entrance doors) make a left from the doors, proceed to the lobby, continue directly across the lobby.
    • Accessible Restrooms: Basement only. (from accessibility entrance doors) After arriving at the lobby, proceed directly across to the elevators. Take the elevator down one floor. The restrooms are located in the back right corner of the basement.
